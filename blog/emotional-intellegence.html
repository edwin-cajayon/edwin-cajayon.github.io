What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is being aware of my own emotions and being able to read the emotions of ther people. Which helps you to dive deeper into intuition.

For me there are 4 components of Emotional Intelligence:

1. Self awareness - how aware you are of what you feeling when and why.

2. Self Management- How actively you're able to manage you emotions and behaveiour with purpose and intent.

3. Social awareness - How aware you are of what others are feeling when and why.

4. Social Management - How actively you're able to act to address other people's emotions effectively and intentionally.

At its core, Its about understanding and managine emotions, both my own andothers, in a way that strenghthens relationships instead of wrecking them. It's not just being empathetic and sensitive. its knowing when to express emotions, when to step back. and knowing how to navigate tough situations without letting emotions run wild.

How is EQ Different to IQ?

Think EQ and IQ like two different types of "smartness": EQ - is about understanding emotions- both my own and other people's. It helps you handle feelings, get along with others, and react the right way in different situations. IQ - is about how well you think, solve problems and understand things like math, logic, and facts. It helps you do well in school, figure out puzzles, and learn new things quickly. So, IQ is like being good at solving a Rubik's Cube, and EQ is like knowing when to cheer up a sad friend. both are important in life.

Why EQ is important ?

EQ is VERY important in our life. As a friend - It helps you understand their feelings and know when they're happy, sad or upset - It helps you to become a good listener and support them when they need help - It helps you avoid unneccesary fights by staying calm and solving problems instead of overreacting. - Its helps you make strong, lasting friendships because people feel comfortable around you. As an employee - It helps you work well with others, which is important in any job. - It helps you stay calm under pressure, making it easier to handle stress and deadlines. - It helps you to communicate clearly, avoids misunderstandings. - It helps you to handle critism well, using feedback to improve instead of getting upset. - It helps you become a great leader because people trust and respect those who understand emotions. In short, Eq helps you build good relationships, avoid drama, and succeed in life both personally and professionally.
