What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

Rest/Sleep

Just like weightlifting the muscles grow on the rest days not during the training sessions. same with our brain when trying to learn.

Repetition

The use it or lose it principle, if you want to be good or get better at something do it all the time. because when the neural connection isn't actively used it can weaken or diminish over time. so coding 1 hour a day every week is better than coding 7 hours in one day.

Growth Mindset

Growth mindset what is it? why is it relevant?

Growth mindset is the belief that abilities and intelligence can develop through effort, learning and persistence.
its the opposite of fixed mindset, where people see skills as innate and unchangeable.
Its important as it helps you focus on your own improvement rather than focusing on reaching a goal. its about trusting and enjoying the process.
