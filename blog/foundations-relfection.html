Foundations Reflection

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the Human Skills learning?

First, human skills helped me focus more on listening, learning and collaborating with others rather than needing to appaer capable all the time.
Second, being kinder to myself when it comes to learning. Struggling with javascript really challenged my confidence. human skills taught me to be more compassionate with my self and to see growth as a process not a race.
Lastly, I realised I tend to compare my progess to other. When i saw others picking up the thingds faster, I felt behind or not good enough. Human skills helped me catch those thoughts and remind myself that everyone learns differently.

What has surprised you the most about the Human Skills learning?

What surprised me the most is how impactful it is. In learning and In life. That to be successful its not all tech stuff.
